How It Works

Three steps from curiosity to publishable research

1

Choose an Experiment

Pick from 16 research simulations spanning behavioral ecology, population dynamics, genetics, marine biology, and more.

2

Run the Simulation

Observe animal behavior, manipulate variables, and collect data in immersive browser-based field environments powered by HTML5 Canvas.

3

Analyze & Publish

Export your data, run statistical analyses, write up your results, and submit to the Borchard Labs undergraduate research archive.

Who It's For

Research experience shouldn't depend on geography, wealth, or connections

🎓

Undergrads Without Lab Access

Students at universities with limited research infrastructure who want real experience before applying to graduate programs.

💼

Working Students

Students who support themselves or their families and can't commit to unpaid research positions or off-campus internships.

🌍

Remote & Commuter Students

Students with long commutes or online coursework who can't spend extra hours on campus for traditional lab work.

🔬

Interdisciplinary Explorers

STEM majors curious about fields outside their specialty—a chemistry major who wants to try ecology, or a pre-med student interested in evolutionary biology.
